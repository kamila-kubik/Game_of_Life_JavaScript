Welcome to the Game of Life!

Game rules

Your goal is to keep cells alive and ensure reproduction. At the beginning you can see randomly selected cells, which after click on "play" button, start changing and moving. You can stop the game anytime thanks to click "pause" button, you can also return to the start, only need to click "reset" button.

How to cells reproduce or die? If the cell is alive and:

  • has only 1 neighbour - it dies
  • has more than 3 neighbours - it dies
  • has more than 2 and less than 3 neigbours - it is still alive

If dead cell has 3 and more neighbours - it comes alive. You can create new living cells throught mouse move over the board.

Good luck!
